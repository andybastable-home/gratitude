Hand-drawn boxes use an SVG turbulence/displacement filter on each card's outline (three seeds + tiny rotations, cycled), so no two neighbours match — text stays crisp because only the border is filtered. Cheap enough for a screenful on the Pixel 8a, and fully offline. Hero motifs are simple vendored SVG (blossom, leaves) that fade into the paper. Note on motifs: I matched them to the real month — May reads as blossom, so I'd slot sunflowers into Late Summer (Jul–Sep); say the word if you'd rather sunflowers here. Each remaining season still needs its motif (Spring shoots, Advent candle/star, Winter snowflakes). Happy to tune wobble strength, motif density, or drop the timestamp entirely.
