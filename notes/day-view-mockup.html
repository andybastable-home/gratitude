Colour comes from season + category, not from shouting. The date and the + take the season's accent; each category heading is inked in its own muted hue with a faintly tinted card and a 3px edge. Body text is warm ink, never pure black. Grouped by category, timestamped within. The + floats bottom-right for one-handed reach. Notice the category colours stay constant while the whole canvas shifts season to season — that rotation is the app's signature.
